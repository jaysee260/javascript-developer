About Me

Hello. My name's Juan Carlos and I'm a web developer with a focus on JavaScript living in the Greater Boston Area.

From February to August 2017, I attended an intensive Full Stack Web Development Coding Bootcamp where I acquired working knowledge of a number of front and back end web technologies. Now, I am actively looking for an opportunity to put the skills learned to serious and productive use.

I'm (seriously) interested in taking on problems of great complexity and in finding creative and methodical solutions to them. Moreover, I'm strongly attracted to the idea of work that allows for the sharpening of currently possessed skills, as well as for the learning of new ones.

JavaScript by itself is already a fairly versatile language, and there are two main reasons for why I particularly like working with it: the rich package environment afforded by the npm community, and the added versality bestowed by the Node.js platform.
